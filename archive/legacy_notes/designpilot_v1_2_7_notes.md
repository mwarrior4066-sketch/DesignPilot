## Intent
This pass hardens the pack against recurring deck/report architecture failures:
- wayfinding collapse
- dead zones and floating content
- chrome duplication
- dark-theme contrast shock
- overshoot lines
- slide density overload
- repeated rebuild cycles caused by structural debt
