# Brand and Archetype Summary

Primary sources: Industry & Demographic Encompassing Design Toolkit and Design Strategy and Communication Research.

## Core brand logic
- identity should match psychographics and industry trust signals
- archetypes help lock tone, shape, and voice into one coherent system
- semiotics and shape psychology matter before stylistic preference

## Examples
- Ruler: order, control, authority, structure
- Sage: clarity, truth, education, expertise
- Caregiver: support, reassurance, warmth
- Magician: transformation, wonder, possibility
- Creator: invention, craft, vision

## Shape logic
- squares and rectangles -> stability and discipline
- circles and softer curves -> safety, unity, care
- triangles and sharper angles -> motion, tension, disruption
